Here is an 8 day itinerary filled with lots of exotic activities, exquisite food ensuring you have a magical experience.



Day 1: Cebu Adventures Begin



Morning:

  • Breakfast Recommendation: Café Laguna, known for its traditional Filipino breakfast platters (starting at PHP 250)

Activities:

  • Visit Magellan's Cross and the Basilica del Santo Niño for a glimpse of Cebu's history
  • Enjoy a leisurely walk or bike ride along Cebu’s IT Park
  • Canyoneering at Kawasan Falls: Pay on arrival (PHP 1,300). Float, dive, and jump along this thrilling turquoise clear water river trail, ending at the majestic Kawasan Falls
  • Swim/ surf at the beachSnorkeling, swim in clear water with lots of sardines, turtles and whalefish

Dinner:

  • Head to Lantaw Floating Native Restaurant for seafood and local dishes (approx. PHP 500 per meal)


Day 2: Waterfalls Tour in Cebu



Morning:

  • Breakfast at Abaca Baking Company: Indulge in croissants, avocado toast, and fresh coffee (PHP 300–500)
  • Start your Waterfall Adventure:
    • Dao Falls: A scenic trek through lush greenery. Entrance: PHP 60
    • Inambakan Falls: Multi-tiered falls perfect for swimming (PHP 50 entrance)
    • Tumalog Falls: Known for its ethereal, misty views and clear waters (PHP 20)

Dinner:

  • Try House of Lechon for Cebu’s famous roasted pig dish, lechon (meals around PHP 500)
  • Or enjoy dinner at La Vie Parisienne for wood-fired pizzas and desserts (PHP 600)


Day 3: Island Hopping in Coron



Morning:

  • Fly to Coron from Cebu (approx. PHP 2,500–4,500 one-way)
  • Breakfast: Arrive early and grab breakfast at Coffee Kong in Coron Town (PHP 200–300)

Activities:

  • Book an island-hopping tour to visit:
    • Twin Lagoon: Swim or kayak through its crystal-clear waters
    • Barracuda Lake: Known for its unique cliffs
  • Private tour: PHP 2,500–3,000 | Group tour: PHP 1,200–1,500 per person

Dinner:

  • Relish fresh seafood at Lobster King (meals start at PHP 500)


Day 4–7: Island Expedition (Coron to El Nido)



Day 4:

  • Join an expedition cruise with operators like @keelooma (PHP 18,000–20,000 for a multi-day all-inclusive package). Highlights include:
    • Remote lagoons
    • Hidden beaches
    • Local-style cooking on the boat

Day 5-6:

  • Immerse yourself in island life with activities like snorkeling, paddleboarding, and stargazing

Day 7:

  • Arrive in El Nido and check into your accommodation


Day 8: Beach Hopping in El Nido



Morning:

  • Breakfast at El Nido Boutique & Art Café: A variety of healthy options, including smoothie bowls (PHP 250–350)
  • Activities:
    • Relax at Dolarog Beach: Serene and secluded
    • Visit Las Cabanas Beach: Great for sunset views
    • Explore Nacpan Beach: Perfect for swimming and lounging (PHP 100 entrance)
  • Join an El Nido Island Tour (Tour A):
    • Kayak through the Big Lagoon
    • Snorkel in vibrant coral reefs
    • Tour costs: PHP 1,200–1,500 per person (includes lunch)

Lunch:

  • Treat yourself to lunch at Artcafé or Trattoria Altrove, famous for its Italian dishes (PHP 600–800)

Afternoon:

  • Shop for souvenirs at Calle Hama in El Nido town

Dinner:

  • End your trip with a feast at The Nesting Table, offering a mix of Filipino and international cuisines (meals around PHP 700)


Additional Recommendations:

Ziplining:

  • Experience a zipline at Las Cabanas (PHP 600)


Whale Shark Swim in Oslob:

  • A unique encounter for PHP 1,000


Mosque Dimaukom:

  • A beautiful pink mosque


Transport:

  • Use ferries for island transfers (PHP 1,800–2,000 from Coron to El Nido)
