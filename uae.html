Here’s a more detailed 7-day itinerary for UAE travel enthusiasts, packed with meal recommendations, costs, and the beauty of each location. It blends adventure, culture, and relaxation to create an unforgettable experience.



Day 1: Modern Dubai Exploration



Morning:

  • Breakfast at IHOP (Dubai Mall): Start your day having breakfast at IHOP. A variety of options such as pancakes, french toast, waffles, hash browns, burgers, omelets, combos etc (40- 135AED)

Activities:

  • Dubai Mall: (pictured above) Spend time shopping and exploring attractions like the Aquarium and Underwater Zoo
  • China Town Walk: Discover the colorful Chinese-inspired section inside the mall with multiple food options
  • Dubai Fountain Show: Marvel at the synchronized water show near the Burj Khalifa


Evening:

  • Dinner at Parker's: Enjoy their iconic dishes like Truffle Fries or sliders (approx. AED 80 per meal). Don't miss their signature dessert, the Matilda Cake
  • Dessert at Forever Rose Café: Indulge in beautifully presented treats like the famous milk cakes and dubai chocolate. Meet the owner/dubai bling star ebhrahim. (18-48AED)


Day 2: Cultural and Religious Marvels



Morning:

  • Breakfast at Wokyo (Jumeirah): This spot serves Asian-inspired noodle bowls and a range of savory breakfasts (starting at AED 30)

Activities:

  • Quranic Park: A serene park with beautiful Quran recitations. Visit the Glass House (around 10 AED entry) showcasing plants and trees mentioned in the Quran, complete the tour in the park at Cave of Miracles (pictured above), featuring interactive stories of the Prophets

Evening:

  • Global Village: have an unforgettable experience by exploring 90+ country pavilions with cultural displays, music, dancing, clothes, food, and souvenirs. (Entry costs AED 20)




Day 3: Futuristic and Relaxing Day



Morning:

  • Breakfast at IHOP (Palm Jumeirah): They have such a great variety of things, you must visit more than once. Try their omelets, like the Spicy Poblano (AED 65), served with fresh sides

Activities:

  • Museum of the Future: Discover interactive exhibits on technology and sustainability (pictured above) (entry: AED 145)

Dinner:

  • Evening Sunset Cruise: Embark on a dhow cruise with a 3-course meal and entertainment (AED 200 per person via GetYourGuide)




Day 4: Desert Adventure



Morning:

  • Breakfast at Pappa Roti: Relish their famous caramel-topped buns paired with Karak Tea (approx. AED 50)

Activities:

  • Desert Safari: Experience quad biking, sandboarding, and camel rides, barbecue buffet, henna designs, dancing and entertainment. Safari packages range from (AED 150–250) depending on inclusions


Dinner:

  • Barbecue Buffet: End the night with a delicious BBQ meal in the desert camp followed with entertainment and dancing


Day 5: Abu Dhabi Excursion



Morning:

  • Breakfast at Salt Camp: Enjoy gourmet sliders and refreshing lemonade (meals from AED 45)

Activities:

  • Drive to Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque: A stunning symbol of Islamic architecture with free entry (pictured above)
  • Visit Louvre Abu Dhabi: A world-class art museum showcasing global history (entry: AED 63)


Dinner:

  • Cheesecake Factory: Choose from an extensive menu of mains and desserts (AED 80–120)


Day 6: Nature and Thrills



Morning:

  • Breakfast at Eggspectation: stylish egg focused breakfast (50-100 AED)

Activities:

  • Aquaventure Waterpark: Swim with dolphins, snorkel with sharks or try thrilling water slides. (Entry: AED 299)
  • Relax at JBR Beach: Enjoy jet skiing or parasailing(pictured above, prices start at AED 250)

Dinner:

  • Dave’s Hot Chicken: Famous for spicy chicken sliders and tenders (AED 60 for a meal)


Day 7: Floral Wonderland



Morning:

  • Breakfast at Asak Supermarket Café (Sharjah): Enjoy a fresh, affordable breakfast platter (for under AED 40)

Activities:

  • Miracle Garden: Stunning floral display (pictured above). An ethereal experience: capture Instagram-worthy photos and have a stroll. (AED 55 entry)
  • Cycling at Al Qudra: Rent a bike (AED 50 for 2 hours) and enjoy the peaceful desert views and flowers. You'll meet lots of lovely people along the way, a perfect way to end the trip

Dinner:

  • Final Meal at Wokyo: End the journey with warm comfort food


Additional information:

  • Budget per day: AED 500–800 (including meals, activities, and transport)
  • Best platforms for tickets and bookings: GetYourGuide and Visit Dubai
  • Transport: Use a taxi service called careem or the metro for city travel; rental cars for Abu Dhabi excursions
